About Us

Dwivedi Group of Companies is a leading conglomerate in the fields of automobile and construction materials. With a strong commitment to quality and innovation, we have been serving our clients with excellence for years.

Our Companies

Our group comprises a range of companies specializing in the automobile industry and construction materials sector. Each company is dedicated to delivering top-notch products and services to meet the evolving needs of our customers. Our Companies are : Dwivedi Enterprises Ltd. , Dwivedi Solar , Dwivedi Construction , Dwivedi Infrastructure and Dwivedi Motors

Automobile Division

Our automobile division is focused on providing high-quality vehicles, ranging from cars to commercial vehicles, along with comprehensive after-sales services. We prioritize safety, performance, and customer satisfaction.

Construction Materials Division

In the construction materials sector, we offer a wide range of top-grade materials including cement, steel, and more. Our products are trusted by builders and contractors for their durability and reliability in various construction projects.
